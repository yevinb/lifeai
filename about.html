How it started

The story behind LifeAI

LifeAI started as a personal project — a simple dashboard to keep track of daily tasks and health goals. But as more features were added, it became something much more: a genuine personal command centre powered by modern AI.

The frustration was simple — too many apps, too much switching, too little time. A task app here, a finance tracker there, a health app on your phone, and emails scattered across tabs. LifeAI was built to solve that by bringing everything into one beautifully designed place.

Today, LifeAI uses cutting-edge AI models to help you plan your day, draft emails, analyse spending, and track your wellbeing — all from a single dashboard that respects your privacy and keeps your data secure.
